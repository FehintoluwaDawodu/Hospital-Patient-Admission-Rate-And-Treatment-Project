Hospital-Patient-Admission-Rate-And-Treatment-Project
This study analyzes the hospital admission records of 10,000 patients across multiple healthcare facilities, with emphasis on medical conditions, admission types, insurance coverage, medication usage, and length of hospital stay. 

## Tools used
- Microsoft Excel

## Project Overview
The analysis aims to determine the frequency and distribution of various medical conditions, evaluate patterns in duration of hospitalization, examine treatment costs, and assess insurance billing practices.

## Findings
- High burden of chronic diseases
- Large elderly population
- High average billing
- Strong revenue base
- 1 in 3 tests abnormal
- Balanced admission types
- Long stays

## Recommendation
- Strengthen chronic disease management.
- Improve diagnostic services.
- Optimize hospital costs.
- Efficiency tracking
- Enhance bed and discharge planning.
